Woodland Classic NorthWest

A limited-edition collaoboration by two seriously cool British brands, these shades from Sunspel and Cubitts have that classic, throwback Gatsby style (we're big fans of that classic Thirties keyhole detail on the bridge). Each pair comes with an exclusive, made-in-England Sunspel-branded wood case and a cotton pouch crafted from Sunspels signature cotton jersey, as well as a lens cleaning cloth, printed with a scenescape of Kings Cross illustrated by Lucy Dalzel.

These stylish blue shades with a rectangular, plastic frames might look simple from the front, but the bright red and blue contrast temples - complete with SeeClearly's signature bee - make these one of the coolest pairs of sunglasses this season. Perfect with a white shirt, shorts and a pair of breezy Rivieras on the beach.
