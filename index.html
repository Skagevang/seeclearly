Who are we?

We are a company called SeeClearly which sells glasses. We are obviously not the only company to do this, but we try to set an example for other buisnesses that beeing enviromentally friendly does not exclude delivering a top notch product and earning money. This is not only reflected in the way we ship and produce the product, but also visible on the design of the site and on the product itself.

What do we do?

We try to make sure that as many parts of our product chain as possible operate carbon neutral. We also run our own project that buys pactches of the Amazon rainforest with the intent of preservation. A percentage of every purchase goes against this project, and if you are not interested in our products you can donate to us. Our progress can be followed at the top of this page.

The rainforest

Satellite data revealed that 5,831 square kilometers of land was cut down or burned in the Brazilian Amazon last year: a 16% increase on the destruction of the previous 12 months.This is the second acceleration in three years, following almost a decade of impressive declines. That suggests the state's efforts are having a diminishing impact which is bad news for Brazil and a worrying trend for the planet.
